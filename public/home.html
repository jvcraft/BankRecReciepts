Powerful Features

Everything you need to reconcile bank statements with your general ledger efficiently and accurately.

Multi-Format Support

Import CSV, Excel, PDF, OFX, and QIF files. Our smart parser handles various date formats and currencies automatically.

Smart Matching

Intelligent algorithm matches transactions by amount, date proximity, and check numbers with configurable tolerance settings.

Visual Dashboard

Clear overview of matched and unmatched transactions with real-time statistics and intuitive navigation.

Manual & Custom Matching

Easily handle exceptions with manual matching and create custom entries when needed for complete control.

Export & Reports

Export to Excel or CSV with comprehensive reports. Print-ready formats for audit documentation.

Secure & Private

Firebase authentication protects your data. Reconciliation history is saved securely to your account.
